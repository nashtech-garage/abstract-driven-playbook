The Problem

  • AI generates code fast
    But often creates architectural mess
  • Business + Technology coupling
    Hard to change, test, and maintain
  • Traditional patterns
    Too complex for AI generation

The ADD Solution

Golden Rule

  • Absolute separation between WHAT (business) and HOW (technology)
  • Business depends only on interfaces
  • Technology wired via DI

The 5 Actors

  • Boundary: External contracts (APIs)
  • Core: Business vocabulary
  • Operators: Business logic
  • Implementations: Technology
  • Bootstrap: Wiring everything
